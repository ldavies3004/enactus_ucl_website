What We Do

Sustainable solutions to individual and community challenges. Long lasting empowerment to beneficiaries. Leading the way to improving people's lives. Taking the opportunity and making ideas happen.

Our Projects

Women's Group

This is a project that has the potential to impact lives for years to come, you will be working with women from vulnerable backgrounds to teach them transferable skills to get into employment and also start their own enterprises.

Food Social Enterprise

This is an exciting project for those that would like to gain experience in consulting. You will be working with the founder of Made in Hackney food social enterprise to review her business model, increase existing revenue streams as well as help to identify new ones.

Anchor House

This project is about empowering the homeless to transform their lives through employment. We are working to develop a sustainable scheme that will serve to give homeless people a chance for years to come.

Enterprise Outreach

Be a part of a project that is shaping a generation of upcoming entrepreneurs. We work with schools to teach young people business and economic principles that they then apply to their own ventures to make a profit!

YES + Camden

This project is about empowering young people through technology. You will be teaching young people how to code to develop websites and apps which they will then apply in apprenticeships with local businesses.

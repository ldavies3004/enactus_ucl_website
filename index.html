Community Counts

This project aims to tackle youth unemployment as well as lack of social cohesion. There are 4 steps to this project: 1. Young people pitch to local businesses about the benefits of workexperience for both them and the employer 2. UCL + carry deliver workshops on employability (cv, cover letter and interview help) and professional conduct 3. The young people carry out their work experience 4. Money from the placements goes towards funding an AQA youth leadership award that allows them to act as youth leaders as well as contributing to UCAS points
